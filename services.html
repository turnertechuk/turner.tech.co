Frequently Asked Questions

Some of the questions we're asked most often.

How do I order a custom PC?

Simply head to our Contact page and tell us your budget, what you'll be using the PC for and any preferences you have. We'll recommend the ideal specification.

Can I choose every component?

Absolutely. Every build is completely bespoke, meaning you have complete control over the specification if you wish.

Do you use brand new parts?

Yes. Unless agreed otherwise, every system is assembled using brand new genuine components from trusted manufacturers.

Do you offer repairs?

Not at the moment. Our current focus is custom PC builds and hardware upgrades, although we hope to introduce repair services in the future.
